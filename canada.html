Explore Opportunities in Canada

Clockwise Immigration Services specializes in helping individuals and families move to Canada with ease and confidence. From visa assistance to settlement services, we are here to guide you every step of the way.

Permanent Residency (PR)

We provide comprehensive support for obtaining Permanent Residency status, ensuring that you meet all eligibility requirements and submit a strong application.

Study in Canada

Our team helps students secure admissions to top Canadian institutions, along with assistance in applying for study permits and scholarships.

Work Opportunities

We assist skilled professionals in finding employment opportunities and acquiring the necessary work permits to start their careers in Canada.

Settlement Services

Our settlement services include finding housing, understanding healthcare, and connecting you with community resources to make Canada feel like home.
